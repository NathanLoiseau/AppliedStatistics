R Code for Socioeconomic Determinants of Life Expectancy: A 2019 Analysis Across U.S. States

Introduction:

In this project we will analyse the factors that affect life expectancy (response variable).The training data from 2018 will be used in different models to predict life expectancy the following year. The model with the least variance from the actual 2019 data will be chosen.

The table bellow represents the dependent and independent variables that will be analyzed and a desciption what they mean. Life Expentancy is the dependent/response variable in this analysis.
